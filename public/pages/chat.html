Great question Amira! Here's a step-by-step method for 3-variable simultaneous equations:

Step 1: Pick any 2 equations, eliminate one variable
Step 2: Pick a different pair, eliminate the same variable
Step 3: You now have 2 equations with 2 unknowns — solve normally
Step 4: Substitute back to find the third variable
