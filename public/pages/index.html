Features

Everything you need to study smarter

Five powerful tools, all working together to help you learn faster and more effectively.

🏠

Study Space

A social feed where students share knowledge, ask questions, and upvote the best answers. AI-validated posts rise to the top automatically.

🎓

Classrooms

Organised courses for every GCSE subject. Browse by year group, filter by subject, and track your progress through structured content.

📝

Smart Notes

A rich text editor built for students — formatting toolbar, an AI assistant panel, and auto-save. Your notes, always at hand.

💬

Study Chat

Group chat rooms for each subject. Type @bitBuddy in any channel to get instant AI help right inside the conversation.

🤖

AI Assistant

A dedicated Claude-powered AI tutor that explains concepts, checks your answers, quizzes you, and helps structure essays for any GCSE subject.

👤

Profile & Progress

Track your study streak, earn Buddy Points, and see your activity across every module — all in your personal profile dashboard.
