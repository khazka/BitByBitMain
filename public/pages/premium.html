Frequently asked questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — you can cancel your Premium or Pro subscription at any time. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period.
Is there a student discount?
bitBuddy Premium is already priced for students at £4.99/month. We also offer a free plan with plenty of features to get you started.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay via our secure payment provider.
Can I switch between plans?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your profile settings. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.
